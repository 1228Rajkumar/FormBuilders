Form Builder :

  • Introduction:
    • Form Builder is a tool that enables users to create various forms and share links of those forms with others. This platform provides a simple and intuitive interface to build forms with various fields. Additionally, it allows users to view the collected data.
  • Sign Up & Sign In:
    • The platform has a secure sign-up and sign-in process that requires users to provide their email address and create a password. The password is encrypted and stored securely to protect users' privacy. Once signed in, users can access their account from anywhere and create as many forms as they need.
  • Form Builder:
    • Form Builder is the core feature of this platform. It allows users to create forms with various fields. Users can customize their forms by adding or removing fields, changing the order of fields.
  • Sharing Forms:
    • Once a form is created, users can share links to the form with others by email, social media. The platform provides a unique URL for each form, making it easy to share and manage.
  • Data Collection & Analysis:
    • The platform collects data from users who fill out the forms and stores it securely in the cloud. Users can view the data collected and analyze it to gain insights into their audience. Additionally, they can download the data as a CSV file for further analysis in other tools.
  • Conclusion:
    • Form Builder & Sharing Platform is a user-friendly tool that enables anyone to create and share forms with ease. With a secure sign-up and sign-in process, form builder, data collection and analysis, and sharing capabilities, this platform provides a complete solution for anyone looking to build and share forms.

Get Started :

1. Create forms from scratch :

  • On the dashboard, look for the "New Form" button and click on it and choose the "Create From Scratch" option.
  • You'll be directed to the form builder interface. Here, you can add fields to your form by dragging and dropping from the field options on the left-hand side of the screen. Field types supported include text, number, date, email and many more.
  • To add a field, simply drag the field type you want to add, and drop it in the center of the screen. You can then edit the field name, description, and options as needed.
  • To make fields mandatory, click on the field in the center of the screen, and you'll see an option to make the field required. Turn this on if you want the user to be forced to answer this field before submitting the form.
  • Finally, to create the form click on the "Build" button in the builder window. Your form will be created and a popup with the QR-code for the form link will show up.
  • You can share the form through various methods, such as a direct link or through QR codes generated within the website.
  • To view the form submissions, navigate to the home in the app, and click on the form you want to view. You'll be able to see all the responses to the form in a table format.
  • You can download the form submissions as a CSV file by clicking the "Download" button in the view submissions window.

2. Create forms by importing CSV file :

  • On the website's main window, click on the "New Form" button.
  • When prompted, select the Import CSV file option.
  • Select and upload the file using drag and drop or the browse button.
  • You will then be prompted to modify any field options that you want to include in the form.
  • Once modified the desired fields, click on the "Build" button to generate the form.
  • The application will then create a form based on your selections, and display a QR-code for the respective form. Also the data from the CSV file is added to our database as form submissions.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) :

    Where can I see the form submissions ?
    To view the form submissions for the form you created just follow the steps:
    [ Home -> Click the form you want to view -> Submissions will be displayed ]
    Can I delete a specific submission from the form submissions ?
    Yes, to delete a specific submission from the form submissions just follow the steps.
    [ Home -> Click the form you want to modify -> Click to select the specific row -> Delete ]
    The data will be successfully deleted.
    Can I change the account password ?
    Yes, to reset the account password follow the steps:
    [ Settings -> Change Password -> Enter the values -> Change ]
    Your account password will be changed successfully.
